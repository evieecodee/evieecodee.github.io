Turtles

5 Turtle Facts

  1. Turtles don't have teeth
  2. Their shells are made of over 50 bones fused together
  3. The largest marine turtle ever found was 2.5 m long, weighing over 900kg
  4. Only around 1 in 1,000 marine turtle hatchlings make it to adulthood
  5. Females return to the same beach they hatched on, to lay their own eggs
